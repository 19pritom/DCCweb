About Us

District Computer Centre (DCC) is a premier government-recognized training institute under the Government of Assam, committed to delivering quality computer education and digital literacy to students, professionals, and citizens of all backgrounds.

We are proud to announce that DCC has successfully completed 11 glorious years of service in the field of computer education and skill development. Our alumni are now working across various sectors including government departments, private enterprises, and self-employment ventures.
